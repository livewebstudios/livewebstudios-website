How to Add Tour Dates

A step-by-step walkthrough for managing your show calendar through the Decap CMS admin panel.

📧
Before you begin — check your email. You should have received an invitation email from Jon Wolf at Live Web Studios. That email contains a link to verify your address and set your password. You can't log in until that's done. Check your spam folder if you don't see it. Once you're set up, you won't need to do this again.
0

Go to Your Admin Panel

Open your browser and go to your website address with /admin added to the end. For example:

yourband.com/admin

Bookmark this URL. You'll use it every time.

The Decap CMS admin landing page. One button. Hard to miss.

1

Log In

Click "Login with Netlify Identity." A login dialog will appear. Enter your email address and the password you set when you accepted the invitation.

Enter your email and password, then click Log in.

Forgot your password? Hit "Forgot password?" right on that screen and it'll email you a reset link.

2

Find Tour Dates in the Dashboard

After you log in, you'll land on the Collections dashboard. This is the main menu for everything your site manages.

Click Site Content in the left column, then click Tour Dates from the list.

Click "Site Content" on the left, then "Tour Dates."

3

Add a New Show

You'll see your current show list. To add a new date, click "Add shows +" on the right side of the SHOWS bar.

Click "Add shows +" to create a new entry. Then scroll down to the bottom — that's where the new blank form opens.

💡
Scroll down after clicking. The new entry form opens at the bottom of the list — below all your existing shows. Scroll all the way down to find it.
4

Fill In the Show Details

Fill in the fields for the new show. Required fields are marked below. The rest are optional — leave them blank if you don't have that info yet.

Field What to Enter
VENUE NAME Required The full name of the venue. e.g., The Stone Pony
CITY / STATE Required City and state. e.g., Asbury Park, NJ
DATE Required Use the date picker or type in MM/DD/YYYY format.
SHOW TIME Optional e.g., 8:00 PM. Leave blank if not confirmed yet.
TICKET / EVENT URL Optional Paste a link to Eventbrite, Facebook event, or venue page.
BUTTON LABEL Optional Text for the ticket button. Defaults to GET TICKETS. Options: MORE INFO · FREE ADMISSION
NOTES Optional Any extra info. e.g., Free Admission · 21+

The new show entry form. Fill in what you have and leave the rest blank.

5

Publish Your Changes

When you're done, scroll back up to the top and click the Publish button. You'll see a green "Entry saved" confirmation in the top right.

Green "Entry saved" toast = you're done. Your site updates automatically within about 30 seconds.

✅
That's it. No FTP. No plugins. No contacting anyone. The site rebuilds itself and the new date is live within about 30 seconds.

Your tour date system is fully customizable. Here's what's already built in — and what can be added.

Old Dates Drop Out Automatically

Past shows disappear from the live site on their own. You don't have to clean anything up.

Shows can be displayed in any order on any page — chronological, featured first, reverse — totally your call.

🧩

Custom Fields

Need a "Doors Time" field? A "Support Act" field? New fields can be added easily without rebuilding anything.

📄

Show Up Anywhere on the Site

The same show data can feed the homepage, a dedicated tour page, or both. One entry, multiple places.
