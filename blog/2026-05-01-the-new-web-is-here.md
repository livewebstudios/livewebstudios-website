## Something Shifted — And Most Business Owners Haven't Noticed Yet

A year ago, the conversation about AI was mostly hype. Chatbots, content generators, stuff that kind of worked and kind of didn't. Today it's different. The tools caught up to the promise, and the result is a fundamentally new way to build and maintain a website.

At Live Web Studios, we've been building websites since 2004. We've seen the shift from static HTML to WordPress, from desktop-first to mobile-first, from keyword stuffing to real SEO. This one feels bigger.

## What "AI-Built" Actually Means

When we say an AI-built site, we're not talking about a chatbot generating your homepage and hoping for the best. We mean a modern, lean stack where:

- The site code is clean, fast, and doesn't carry ten years of WordPress plugin debt
- Performance is built in from the start — not patched on top
- The whole thing lives in GitHub, deploys in seconds, and costs a fraction of what traditional hosting runs

No database. No constant updates. No security vulnerabilities hiding in a plugin you forgot you installed in 2021.

## What This Means for Your Business

Faster load times translate directly to better Google rankings. A lighter site means lower hosting costs. Less complexity means less that can go wrong. And because everything is built intentionally — not assembled from templates — your site actually reflects your business instead of looking like everyone else's.

We're in the middle of transitioning our existing clients to this stack right now. The results so far have been what we expected: faster, cleaner, better.
