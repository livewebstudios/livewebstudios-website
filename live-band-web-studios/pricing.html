Good Questions

What does "prepaid" mean?

You pay for the year upfront. Jon doesn't do monthly invoices. In 20-plus years, no client has had a problem with it.

What if I need to cancel?

Hosting is prepaid — no refunds if you leave early. The site is yours. Jon hands it over clean.

Can I upgrade from Basic to Touring/Pro?

Yes. Call Jon. We'll figure out the difference and build the add-ons.

Who handles updates?

You handle gig dates and set lists through the CMS. Jon handles everything else in your monthly hour. Need more? Just ask — we'll work it out.

Do I need to supply my own photos and videos?

Yes on photos (high-res preferred — Jon will tell you what works). Video embeds come from your YouTube channel. Jon will tell you exactly which ones to point him at.

What's the blog about?

Local SEO. Every show in a new market is a blog post that gets your name in front of people searching for live music in that city. It's the long game. It works.
