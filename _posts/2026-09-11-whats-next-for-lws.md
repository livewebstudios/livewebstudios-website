## It's Been a Year

We kicked off 2026 with a clear goal: move our clients to a better, faster, more sustainable web platform — and build the infrastructure to serve them better for the next decade, not just the next year. That meant migrating sites, redesigning how we handle reporting, building automation into things that used to require manual effort, and starting to think seriously about what a small business AI consulting practice looks like.

Here's where things stand.

## The Migration Campaign

We're in the middle of moving 25-30 client sites from WordPress to a modern AI-built stack. The early results have confirmed what we expected: faster sites, lower hosting costs, dramatically reduced maintenance overhead, and genuinely better SEO performance coming out of the gate.

Not everyone is moving. WooCommerce clients and a handful of others are staying where they are — and that's the right call for them. What everyone is getting, regardless of platform, is monthly reporting: real data, plain-English analysis, and actual recommendations.

## What the Monthly Reports Have Shown Us

Running analytics and search console data across a growing number of client sites has given us a clearer view than we've ever had of what's working in small business SEO right now. Local signals matter more. Content consistency beats content volume. Site speed is a real ranking factor. None of this is surprising — but seeing it consistently across dozens of sites gives it more weight.

We'll continue sharing what the data is telling us in future posts.

## The AI Consulting Practice

This is the next chapter. We're building an offer around helping small businesses figure out what AI can actually do for their specific operation — not the hype version, the practical version. It'll launch in early 2027. If you want to be among the first to hear about it, get on the list.

## Thank You

Twenty-plus years of running this business has taught me that the relationships are what compound. The clients I've worked with for 10 years are better clients than we were a year into working together, because we understand each other. That's what the monthly reports, the calls, and the content like this are all about — staying in relationship, not just in contact.
