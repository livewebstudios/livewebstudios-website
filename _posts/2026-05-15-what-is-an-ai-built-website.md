## Let's Skip the Buzzwords

"AI-built website" sounds like marketing speak. And honestly, a lot of how it gets described out there earns the skepticism. So let me explain it the way I would sitting across from you at a coffee table.

## What Actually Happens

Build a website the traditional way and you're working inside a content management system, usually WordPress, that ships with a pile of pre-built pieces. We configure those pieces, add your content, install plugins for forms and SEO, and that's your site.

Build it the new way and we use AI tools to write the actual code. Clean HTML, CSS, and lightweight JavaScript, made for your business. Think IKEA furniture versus a piece built to fit your room. Both can look great. One fits exactly.

## What AI Is Actually Doing

It helps us move faster through the build. It writes code, checks the code, lets us iterate on layout and content without starting from scratch every time. We're still designing. We're still making the calls. The AI handles what used to be the tedious technical scaffolding.

## What You End Up With

The end result is a site that:

- Loads extremely fast (because there's no database, no plugin overhead)
- Is easy to update and maintain
- Costs less to host over time
- Is built to modern web standards from the ground up

It's not magic. It's just a better process producing a better product.
