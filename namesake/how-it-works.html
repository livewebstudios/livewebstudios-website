The process

Five steps. Start to launch.

No intake maze. No template-library mockups. Jon listens first, then Namesake builds — and you see the real thing before anything goes live.

Tell Namesake about you.

A short, painless intake. Who you are, what you do, what the site needs to pull off. Ten minutes, tops — and you can skip anything you want.

Namesake designs and builds.

Fully custom, from a blank page, on a private staging server. No templates. No mockups pulled from a library. Built by hand, the way it's been done here since 2004.

You take a look.

See the real thing on staging — not a flat picture of it. Ask for changes. Sit with it. When it's right, you give the nod.

It goes live.

Point your domain and you're up. Jon walks you through it himself — an actual conversation, not a PDF and a good-luck.

It's yours.

Manage and update it yourself anytime through a simple editor. And Jon's number stays right on the site — call anytime you want a hand.

What's included

Everything the name carries.

One custom build, top to bottom. Here's what comes with a Namesake site.

  • Fully custom design. Built from scratch, around your name — no templates, no page builders.
  • Custom imagery, illustration & photo work. Original visuals and art direction. Never stock.
  • Animated & video backgrounds. Ambient motion and parallax that make the site feel alive.
  • Responsive on every device. Phone, tablet, laptop, the big monitor — it holds up everywhere.
  • SEO + schema, AI Platformed. Built to be found by Google — and by the AI tools doing the searching now.
  • Editor access. Update your own content anytime — no developer required, no surprise bill.
