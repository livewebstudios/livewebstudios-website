Why Namesake

Built entirely for you.

No templates. No page builders. Every Namesake site is hand-built from a blank page — by Live Web Studios, the same North Jersey studio that's been building the web since 2004.

Fully custom design

No templates. No drag-and-drop. Every pixel is drawn for you — your look, your story, your name front and center.

Custom imagery & illustration

Original visuals, photo work, art direction. Not stock. The images carry your name as carefully as the words do.

Motion that moves

Ambient video, parallax, fluid interaction. A Namesake site feels alive the moment someone lands — that's the whole point.

Built for how people find you now

Clean structure, schema, and SEO from day one — AI Platformed, so the search tools doing the looking actually find you. Including the AI ones.

Yours to run

When it's done, it's yours. Update it through a simple editor anytime — no developer required, no surprise bill to keep it online. And Jon's number is on the site if you ever want a hand.

The process

Simple, and built around you.

No intake maze. No mockups pulled from a template library. Jon listens, then Namesake builds — custom, on a private staging server, until it's exactly yours. Five clean steps, start to launch.
