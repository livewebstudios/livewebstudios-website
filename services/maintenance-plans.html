What's Included

Everything Your Website Needs. Nothing You Have to Think About.

A maintenance plan means your site is monitored, protected, backed up, and reported on every single month — without you lifting a finger.

Managed Hosting

Your site lives on our managed VPS server — LiteSpeed-powered, fully isolated, and optimized for speed. No shared servers, no noisy neighbors, no downtime from someone else's problems.

Daily Backups

Your site is backed up every day and stored offsite on Backblaze. If anything ever goes wrong — whether it's a hack, a bad update, or human error — we restore it. Fast. No data loss, no panic.

Security Monitoring

We actively monitor your site for malware, unauthorized access, SSL certificate status, and known vulnerabilities. Security patches and hardening updates are applied proactively — not after something breaks.

Monthly SEO Report

Every month you receive a branded performance report covering uptime statistics, security scan results, traffic overview, and keyword ranking updates. You stay informed without having to ask.

Content Edits

Need to update a phone number, swap an image, add a new service, or change business hours? We handle small content edits as part of your plan — no hourly billing, no tickets, no waiting.

Priority Support

Maintenance clients go to the front of the line. If something breaks, you hear from us first. Most issues are resolved within the same business day — often within hours.

Pricing

Custom Plans for Every Size Business

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all maintenance pricing. A 5-page brochure site has different needs than a 50-page e-commerce store with weekly inventory updates. We build maintenance plans around what your site actually needs — not a menu of tiers designed to upsell you on things you don't use.

Every plan includes hosting, daily backups, security monitoring, monthly reporting, and content edits. Larger plans add faster response times, additional edit hours, quarterly strategy calls, and enhanced SEO reporting.
